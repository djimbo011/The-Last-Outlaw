The Chronicle of Sherwood

The Legend Begins

In the heart of Sherwood Forest, where ancient oaks whisper secrets older than the crown, a single outlaw stands against the tide of tyranny. The Sheriff has sent his armies to burn the woodland to ash — but Robin Hood does not flee. He fights.

This is a survival arena where every second is a battle. Every tree sways with living wind, every character walks with weight and purpose, and the forest breathes with ambient motion. You are not playing a spreadsheet — you are standing in a world that wants you dead.

“It is not for a man to be proud of his birth, nor of his power; but of his deeds and his heart.” — The spirit of Sherwood.

Stage & Difficulty

Each map lasts 12 minutes (~29 stages). You advance by surviving — each stage lasts 25 seconds. A boss appears every 4 minutes (3 total). When a boss spawns, all small enemies vanish — it becomes a pure 1v1 duel to the death.

Difficulty scales gradually: enemy HP increases ~10% per minute, enemy damage +2% every 2 minutes (max +10%), and an additional +8% HP per 5 player levels. Spawn rates accelerate slowly. After 3 minutes, Armored Knights join the fray. Survive all 12 minutes to achieve victory!

The World — Sherwood Forest

The arena is Sherwood Forest — dense oaks with swaying canopies, mossy boulders worn smooth by centuries, fallen logs covered in lichen, wildflowers dotting the moss. Trees form a living wall at the edges, impossible to pass. Sunlight filters golden through the canopy. The wind never stops: trees sway, leaves drift, the forest breathes.

Scattered cover — trees, rocks, bushes, logs — blocks movement and breaks line of sight from crossbowmen. Use them. Circle-strafe. Disappear. Reappear behind a different tree. The forest is your ally as much as your battlefield.

Core Mechanics

Combat: All attacks are automatic. Weapons fire on cooldown, targeting the nearest enemy. Your job is positioning and survival.

Movement is survival: Enemies chase relentlessly. Standing still = death. Always be moving, kiting in circles, using obstacles to break their path.

Contact damage: Touching an enemy deals damage and grants 0.8s invulnerability (character flashes). Use this to escape crowds.

Ranged enemies: Crossbowmen keep distance and fire blue bolts. Dodge by changing direction unpredictably.

The Leveling System

XP & Hearts: Every enemy drops an XP gem. Walk near gems to collect them (or get Arrow Retrieval for magnet range). Each gem grants XP. Additionally, there is a 5% chance (15% for bosses) to drop a red heart that heals 30% of your max HP — grab it fast!

Leveling up: Each level +5 max HP and heals 10. XP needed grows ~18% per level.

The 5-Level Choice: Every 5 levels (5, 10, 15, 20...), the game pauses and shows 3 random upgrade cards. Pick one:

  • New Weapons — Add a weapon you don't own (max 4 total).
  • Weapon Upgrades — Improve existing weapons (more damage, projectiles, fire rate). Up to 5 levels each.
  • Abilities — Passive boosts (speed, HP, damage, lifesteal, etc.). Most stack up to 3x. Arrow Volley is once-only.

Strategy: Early game — grab new weapons. Mid game — Multi Shot + damage upgrades. Late game — HP and regen to survive bosses.

Boss Encounters

A boss spawns every 4 minutes if none is active. A dramatic banner announces the boss (no background, slow blink). When a boss appears, ALL small enemies are cleared — it is a pure 1v1 duel! Each boss has 3 devastating special attacks. Bosses have massive HP, hit hard, and drop large XP bounties — often 1-2 free levels. Survive 12 minutes to win!

Boss strategy: Never fight in a corner. Keep moving in wide circles. Use orbiting weapons for chip damage, piercing/burst for direct hits. If HP drops low, create distance and rely on lifesteal or regen.

Pro Tips

  • Keep moving. A stationary outlaw is a dead outlaw.
  • Collect XP gems aggressively. XP = levels = power. Walk close to gems to pick them up!
  • Stack synergies. Sharpshooter + Quick Draw + Arrow Volley = devastating firepower.
  • Prioritize crossbowmen. They chip HP from range.
  • Use obstacles. Circle-strafe trees to split crowds.
  • Don't panic at level-up. The game pauses. Read all 3 cards.
  • Always finish bosses. The XP bounty is worth the risk.

Difficulty Scaling

The forest gets deadlier over time. Enemy HP and damage scale ~10% per minute. Spawn rate increases gradually. After 3 minutes, Armored Knights join. Bosses grow stronger each cycle. Survive 12 minutes to achieve victory and become a Legend of Sherwood!
